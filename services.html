What if we don't know which service we need?
Start with an AI Audit. We'll assess your situation and recommend the right service. It's only £2-5K and gives you clarity.
Can you work with small teams or just enterprises?
Both. We work with 5-person startups and 50,000-person organisations. The approach changes, but the methodology is the same: understand, build, validate, scale.
What if the implementation doesn't work?
We iterate. If something isn't working, we diagnose why and adjust. Your success is our success, so we stay engaged until you get results.
Do you build everything from scratch?
No. We use existing tools (Claude, GPT-4, etc.) and integrate them into your workflows. Custom development only when off-the-shelf doesn't fit.
How long does a typical engagement take?
Audits: 2 weeks. Coaching: ongoing (1-12 months). Development: 6-12 weeks. Rollout: 3-6 months. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.
Can we start with a coaching engagement?
Absolutely. Start wherever makes sense. You can always upgrade or add services as you go.
