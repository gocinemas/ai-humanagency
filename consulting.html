Questions

What's the typical engagement look like?
It depends on what you need. An audit might be 2-4 weeks. A strategy piece might be 6-8 weeks. Implementation can be longer depending on scope. We'll be clear about timeline and cost before we start.
Do you work with startups or just enterprises?
Both. Startups building AI into their product. Enterprises figuring out how to use it. The size doesn't matter as much as the question: is this actually worth solving?
What if we don't know where to start?
That's the most common situation. We start with a conversation. You tell us what's frustrating you. We help you figure out if AI is actually the answer, and if it is, what the first move should be.
Do you build the AI solutions?
We guide the strategy and help you build or find the right team to execute. We focus on the thinking part. Making sure you're solving the right problem in the right way.
How do you price?
Project-based, not hourly. We'll talk through what you need, give you a clear number, and get to work. No surprises.
